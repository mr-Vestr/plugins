## **⚠️  ADB Lite — a plugin that allows you to install plugins on your device remotely via Wi-Fi**

---

### **🌟 Features:**


- Instantly install plugins from PC to phone over Wi-Fi;

- Fast device discovery and pairing via mDNS;

- No need to deal with root and ADB;

- Convenient server control from settings: enable/disable with a beautiful animated button;

- Device management: view connected devices, rename and delete them from the saved list;

- Flexible settings: auto-approve connections, auto-install plugins, notification control.

---

### **⚙️ Settings:**


- Ability to enable / disable the server in the [Manage ADB Lite](https://t.me/exteraSettings?p=adb_lite&s=manage_adb_lite) menu;

- View [Saved Devices](https://t.me/exteraSettings?p=adb_lite&s=saved_devices) — rename, view information and delete;

- [Auto-approve connections](https://t.me/exteraSettings?p=adb_lite&s=auto_approve_connections) — automatically accept requests from previously connected devices;

- [Auto-install plugins](https://t.me/exteraSettings?p=adb_lite&s=auto_install_plugins) — automatically install received plugins without showing a menu;

- Notification settings — enable or disable notifications for [pairing](https://t.me/exteraSettings?p=adb_lite&s=notification_pair), [disconnection](https://t.me/exteraSettings?p=adb_lite&s=notification_disconnect) and [installation](https://t.me/exteraSettings?p=adb_lite&s=notification_install);

- [Main plugins menu button](https://t.me/exteraSettings?p=adb_lite&s=show_drawer_menu) — adds a settings button to the main plugins menu;

- [Settings menu button](https://t.me/exteraSettings?p=adb_lite&s=show_settings_menu_button) — adds a settings button to the Telegram settings menu;

- [Plugin language](https://t.me/exteraSettings?p=adb_lite&s=plugin_language) — choose the interface language (Russian, English or system);

- [Particles in settings](https://t.me/exteraSettings?p=adb_lite&s=particles_in_settings) — enable animated particles in the settings menu.

---

### **💯 How to use:**


1. Open the ADB Lite settings and go to "Manage ADB Lite";

2. Tap the round button to start the server;

3. Download ADB Lite Desktop on your computer from [GitHub](https://github.com/mr-Vestr/adb-lite-desktop);

4. Open the app on your computer, go through a quick setup and connect to your phone;

5. Now you can instantly install plugins on your phone!

---

### **✨ A little more:**


- The plugin uses a built-in HTTP server on your phone — no root or ADB required;

- All data is transferred over your local Wi-Fi network only;
